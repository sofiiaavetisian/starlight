How it works

We use Two‑Line Element (TLE) data to describe orbits and SGP4 to compute a satellite’s current position. Then we convert those coordinates to latitude and longitude on Earth. It’s accurate enough for learning and exploration, and we’ll clearly explain assumptions along the way.
